Our Story

Basti Ki Pathshala Foundation was founded under the Indian Societies Act of 1860 with a powerful vision: to transform the lives of slum children through education. Inspired by personal experiences and a passion for social justice, Sunita began this journey with a team of dedicated individuals who believed in every child's potential, regardless of circumstance.

From modest beginnings, we’ve grown into a beacon of hope—witnessing shy children become confident learners and communities unite for a better future. Every step forward has strengthened our commitment to breaking barriers and empowering through education.

We are deeply grateful to our volunteers, donors, and partners who continue to support our mission. Our story is still unfolding—rooted in hope, resilience, and the belief that every child deserves the chance to thrive.

Our Mission

we are committed to breaking educational barriers in slum communities by providing quality, inclusive learning opportunities. Our mission is to empower every child with the knowledge and support they need to build a brighter, more equitable future.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every child, no matter their social or economic background, has the opportunity to learn, grow, and thrive. Our vision is to create an inclusive society where education empowers every child to reach their full potential.
